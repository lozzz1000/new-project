price 70300$+

The Duality Pickaxe was released in the Christmas 2022 update and is a special pickaxe. It is obtained through a secret quest, check Obtaining to learn the steps. The pickaxe is dual-wielded and slightly glows when you pull it out. The Duality Pickaxe now deals 1.2 damage after a buff in patch.a of 0.4v.

The Duality Pickaxe will glow every hit. After 12 hits, the Duality Pickaxe summons a large spinning clone of itself and damages the surrounding ore pieces of the node. The glows will stop appearing during the cooldown of the ability.
